Lucifer

About

Lucifer is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The character was created by Neil Gaiman, Sam Kieth, and Mike Dringenberg, and first appeared in The Sandman #4 (August 1989).

Lucifer is the former ruler of Hell, who abdicated his throne and came to Earth. He is a powerful and charismatic figure, who is often portrayed as a rebel and an outsider.

  • Name: Lucifer Morningstar
  • Alias: The Lightbringer, The Devil
  • Occupation: Former ruler of Hell, nightclub owner
  • Powers: Superhuman strength, speed, and durability; immortality; teleportation; shapeshifting; reality warping
